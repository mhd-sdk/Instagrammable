âœï¸ 2. Caption Structure (The H.V.A. Method)
To maximize retention and engagement, use the following structure:

The Hook (1st Line):

Must be visible before the "more" button.

Goal: Stop the scroll.

Examples: "The truth about...", "Stop doing [X] if you want [Y]", "3 tools that saved me 10 hours."

The Value (Body):

Whitespace is key: Use single-sentence paragraphs.

Use bullet points or emojis to list information.

Tone: Conversational, bold, and punchy.

The Call to Action (CTA):

One clear instruction per post.

Examples: "Comment 'READY' to get the link," "Save this for your next session," or "Tag a friend who needs to hear this."
